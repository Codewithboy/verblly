Terms & Conditions

Registration & fees -:

Registration is confirmed only after the completion of full payment.

The course fees can be paid online via UPI/bank transfer/Paytm.

Anyone aged 10 years or above can register for our courses.

The company reserves the right to postpone or cancel a batch in case the minimum numbers of students required for a batch have not registered.

The course fee includes all the study material required.


Attendance -:

We track every student’s attendance. In case you are unable to attend class, please try to inform us well in advance. 

It is advised to join the class 5 minutes before the scheduled time.

The study material and class link shall be provided to the students 48/24 hours prior to the start date.

Student is expected to attend all the classes regularly.


Change/Withdrawal of Batch -:

Fees once paid will NOT be refunded under any circumstances unless the course is cancelled by the company. 

Transfer from one batch to another batch of the same level will only be allowed only once on the basis of availability of seats in the preferred batch.


Online Class Etiquette -:

Decent behavior is expected when you communicate with both your classmates and mentors. 

The sessions will be conducted on the Google classroom/Google meet/Zoom/ apps. Try as much as possible to join the class with a stable internet connection.

We strongly recommended to join the class with a PC/MAC/Laptop instead of a mobile device for your easy learning experience.

Make sure you have no background noise as possible to improve your learning experience.
